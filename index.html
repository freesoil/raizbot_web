Ranch • Soil • Robotics

AI robots verifying carbon credits with trust and precision.

Billions meant for climate action fail to reduce emissions because credits are opaque, untrusted, and often unverifiable. Ranchers are among the largest natural sinks—but without continuous, trusted measurement, their real carbon value is ignored or discounted. Raizbot changes that with verifiable data from the ground up.

Problem

The carbon market is broken.

Credits rely on infrequent surveys and coarse estimates. Funds intended to cut emissions miss the mark. Land stewards lose recognition for real sequestration.

Solution

Continuous, verifiable field data.

Raizbot deploys autonomous ground and aerial robots to measure soil carbon and vegetation health in real time. Every reading is immutably tagged with location, method, and system health—restoring trust and making credits tradable.

From roots to registries

We connect underground measurements and above-ground growth to audit-grade reports ready for verification frameworks.

Technology

Physical AI built for the field.

Deep soil sampling

Autonomous coring with RTK positioning and adaptive force control ensures reliable depth across varied soils.

  • ROS2 edge control on NVIDIA Orin/Xavier
  • Real-time obstruction detection
  • Closed-loop motor control

Vision-based biomass tracking

RGB/multispectral imaging analyzed by efficient neural backbones quantifies vegetation growth and above-ground carbon.

  • Context-aware inference
  • On-device + cloud analytics
  • Uncertainty-aware estimates

Health & provenance

Pre-sample checks and anomaly detection; each measurement cryptographically logged with full provenance.

From measurements to markets

Standardized outputs map cleanly to verification frameworks—turning measured impact into trusted credits.
